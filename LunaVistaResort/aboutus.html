About ZitowResort

ZitowResort is a relaxing tropical getaway designed for travelers who want comfort, adventure, and beautiful ocean views. Our resort offers modern rooms, relaxing beach activities, and unforgettable island experiences for you, and your family and friends!.

Located in Lian, Batangas, enjoy the near crystal clear waters and scenic landscapes, ZitowResort provides guests with a peaceful environment perfect for families, couples, and friends.

Visitors can enjoy swimming, island tours, sunset dining, and comfortable accommodations that make every stay memorable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide guests with a relaxing and enjoyable vacation while delivering excellent service and unforgettable experiences.
